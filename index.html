About me

Hey! My name is Sean, I'm a trained mechanical engineer turned software engineer. In my day job I work on enterprise java web application using Spring, but in the evening... when no one is looking... I don my front-end mask and enjoy playing with ReactJS, responsive web-design and progressive web applications. I'm always tinkering with new tech/projects and occasionally write about fun/interesting things that I stumble across.

When I'm not coding I'm usually nursing an injury from playing soccer like I'm still 18, snowboarding (when I can), hiking or the gym. I love to travel and try to visit at least one new country a year. Originally I'm from New England but now I call Washington, DC home. Feel free to reach out to me on Twitter or linkedIn if you ever want to chat.
